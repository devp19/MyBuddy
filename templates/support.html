FREQUENTLY ASKED
How does this work?
By using voice interpretation through the google API, into the plushy, we use OpenAI's api for GPT integration which reads in voice to text data and dynamically populates your journal! We temporarily store your speech-to-text data in a dump JSON file which is then sent off to the API.

What makes our responses so human-like?
Through a unique prompt-based method, often known to repersonalize the GPT-model, we give it a base prompt of what the AI system should behave like. This personality is sent along with each user-entry making sure that the model never fails to give a non-humane response.

What made our team work on this?
With mental health issues becoming a major problem in this day and age especially with technological advancements in artificially intelligent sectors such as beauty, social-media and deepfakes, it has become a prevalent issue. With our project, we took into consideration that one may not seek out to a therapist or anyone else due to the fear of being judged. Being able to talk to an inanimate object such as a plushy, it gives you a stronger connection while a GPT-integrated script talks you through all while journaling.
